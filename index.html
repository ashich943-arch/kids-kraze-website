Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents usually ask before their first visit.

What ages is Kids Kraze for?

Our playland is designed for kids aged 3–12. Younger toddlers are welcome too, with parent supervision.

Are socks required?

Yes — grip socks are required for everyone on the play equipment, for hygiene and safety. You can buy a pair at the counter if needed.

Can parents stay and watch?

Absolutely! We have comfortable seating and a café so parents can relax while the kids play.

Can we bring outside food?

Outside food isn't allowed, but our in-house café has snacks, meals and drinks. For birthday parties, you're welcome to bring a cake.

How do I book a birthday party?

Use the “Request Booking” button on this page, or WhatsApp us at 0303 7881100. An advance payment confirms your date and slot.

Is parking available?

Yes, parking is available right by the main gate at Al-Qamar Garden.
